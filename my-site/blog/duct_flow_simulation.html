Potential Flow (Inviscid, Irrotational)
The velocity field is derived from a stream function ψ satisfying ∇²ψ = 0. In the contraction, streamlines converge → speed increases → pressure drops (Bernoulli). At the throat, speed is maximum and static pressure is minimum. In the diffuser, streamlines diverge → speed decreases → pressure recovers. Particles are animated as passive tracers advected by the velocity field.
