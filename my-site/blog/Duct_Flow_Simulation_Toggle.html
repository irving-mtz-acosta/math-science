Potential Flow (Inviscid, Irrotational)
Velocity is derived from continuity: U(x) = Q / h(x). In the contraction, streamlines converge → speed increases → pressure drops (Bernoulli). At the throat, speed is maximum and pressure is minimum. In the diffuser, streamlines diverge → speed decreases → pressure recovers.

Uniform mode: particles are placed on a regular grid — you can clearly see the streamlines squeezing together and the acceleration at the throat.
Random mode: particles are seeded randomly — gives a more realistic "dye injection" appearance.
